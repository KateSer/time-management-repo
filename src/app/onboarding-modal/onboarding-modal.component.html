Welcome to Time Management App

This app is designed to help you organize your ideas and tasks effectively. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Enter Your Ideas:

    Use the "Add" button to enter all your ideas or tasks that you want to prioritize. Each task should have a name which is required.

  2. Assign Urgency and Importance:

    For each task, assign whether it is urgent or not. Assign whether it is important or not. These values help in making informed decisions about your tasks.

  3. Optional Due Date:

    You can also add a due date to each task to help you sort and manage your tasks better. This is optional, but it can be useful for tracking deadlines.

  4. Manage Your Tasks:

    You can:

    • Edit Tasks: You can edit any task if you need to make changes.
    • Delete Tasks: If you change your mind about a task, you can delete it.
    • Clear All Tasks: Use the "Clear All" button to remove all tasks and start fresh.
  5. View and Filter Your Tasks:

    Navigate the top menu buttons to filter your tasks based on their priority:

    • Do It Now: View tasks that are both important and urgent.
    • Do Next: View tasks that are important but not urgent.
    • Do Later: View tasks that are not important but urgent.
    • Delete: View tasks that are neither important nor urgent.

    This helps you focus on what really matters and manage your time efficiently.

Let's Get Started!

Follow these steps, prioritize your tasks, and stay organized. Enjoy managing your time effectively with our app!
